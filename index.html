Curtains up!

The game

You are a stagehand at the local theatre and it is your responsibility to open the curtains before each act.

How to play

  • Open the curtains by clicking on at least three connecting curtain elements of the same color in order to remove them.
  • The more curtain elements are removed at once, the more applause you'll get.
  • Advance to the next act by opening the curtains fully.
  • Press 'H' for a hint if you're stuck.
  • Press 'S' to shuffle the remaining blocks.
  • Restart the current act by pressing 'R'.
  • There are dedicated buttons for these actions below the stage as well.
